U.S. Job Market Box Plot: Salary Distributions Across Experience Levels (2023-2024)

This box plot explores how salary distributions vary by experience level, revealing both central trends and spread across roles. Median salaries increase steadily from Internship to Executive roles, with significantly higher salary variability at senior levels like Director and Executive, reflecting broader compensation ranges. Notably, the consistency in mid-level roles contrasts sharply with the high dispersion among leadership roles, hinting at factors like industry, company size, or negotiation influencing top-end salaries.
