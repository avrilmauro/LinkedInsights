U.S. Interactive Job Sector Network (2023–2024)

This interactive network lets you explore how various sub-industries relate to broader job sectors. Use the dropdown menu to select a sector — the graph will update to show sub-industries within that category. You can zoom in and out and move the nodes around for better viewing at your desire. Each node's size and color reflect the average salary in that sub-industry: larger and lighter nodes typically indicate higher pay. This tool helps you quickly identify salary trends and opportunities across different parts of the job market.
