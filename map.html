U.S. Job Market Map: Salary, Sector, and Skills by State and County (2023–2024)

This interactive map provides a detailed view of the U.S. job market based on LinkedIn job postings from 2023–2024. Users can toggle between two layers—state-level and county-level—using the layer control in the top-right corner. Each region is shaded according to its average salary, with a color gradient that highlights geographic disparities in compensation. Hovering over a state or county reveals a tooltip showing the region’s name and its average salary. Clicking on a region brings up a richer popup with additional insights, including the median salary, total number of job postings, the top hiring sector, the percentage of jobs in that sector, and the top three most common skills found in job listings from that area. This tool enables users to explore spatial trends in compensation, industry dominance, and skill demand across the country.
